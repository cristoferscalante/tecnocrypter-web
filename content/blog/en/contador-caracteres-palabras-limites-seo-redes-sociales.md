# Strategic writing: Tools to count words and respect SEO limits

When creating digital content (whether writing persuasive copy for social networks or writing meta tags to optimize the SEO of an article), the length of your texts is not optional.

Respecting length limits ensures that your message is delivered complete and with visual impact.

### The key limits of SEO and Marketing

* **Google Meta Title:** It should be between 50 and 60 characters. Exceeding this limit causes Google to clip your title with ellipses (\`...\`), decreasing the CTR.
* **Meta Description:** The recommended range is between 150 and 160 characters.
* **Digital Advertising (Google/Facebook Ads):** Ad titles and descriptions have strict character limits that prevent publication if they exceed a single letter.
