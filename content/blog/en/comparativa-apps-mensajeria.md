# Comparison of secure messaging apps in 2025

## Summary
We compare the main messaging apps (Signal, Telegram, Threema, Session) evaluating E2E encryption, open code and identification requirements, to help you choose the most secure option in 2025.

**Keywords:** secure messaging apps, Signal, Telegram, security comparisons

---
## Introduction

Privacy matters more than ever. Choosing the right app can make the difference between protecting or exposing your conversations.


## Apps analyzed
- **Signal**: E2E encryption by default, open source.
- **Telegram**: encryption only in secret chats.
- **Threema**: pays, but without the need for a phone number.
- **Session**: decentralized approach, without metadata.


---
## Comparison
| App | E2E Encryption | Open source | Requires number |
|-----|--------------|----------------|--------------|
| Signal | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Telegram | Only in secret chats | Partial | Yes |
| Threema | Yes | Partial | No |
| Session | Yes | Yes | No |


---
## Conclusion
For maximum security: Signal or Session. For privacy/usability balance: Threema. Telegram falls behind if not used correctly.
