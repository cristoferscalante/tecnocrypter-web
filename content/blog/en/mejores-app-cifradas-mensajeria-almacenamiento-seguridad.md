# The best encrypted messaging and storage apps in 2026: Security comparison

Today, privacy is one of the most valuable assets on the internet. The market is flooded with software that promises to protect your conversations and information in the cloud. However, not all solutions meet the necessary cryptographic standards. We analyze the best **encrypted apps** (or *encrypted apps*) for messaging and storage that you should use in 2026 to protect your sensitive information.

To evaluate the real security of an application, we must look beyond commercial advertising and audit its technical development specifications.

### Comparison of the Best Encrypted Apps in 2026

#### 1. Security Instant Messaging
* **Signal:** Still the industry gold standard. Uses the prestigious *Signal Protocol* to encrypt calls, video calls and texts. Being open source, its cryptography is constantly audited by independent researchers. Does not record conversation metadata.
* **Session:** An excellent option for those looking for maximum anonymity. It does not require a phone number or email for registration, and routes encrypted messages through a decentralized network of nodes (similar to Tor) called *Lokinet*.

#### 2. Secure Cloud Storage
* **Proton Drive:** Switzerland-based provider offering secure storage with end-to-end encryption by default. Its zero-knowledge architecture prevents data from being inspected or handed over to third parties.
* **Cryptee:** A minimalist and secure platform for storing sensitive notes and photos. Encrypt all information on the client side (on your browser or device) before sending it to remote servers.

The use of tools with audited code and strong encryption at the source is the only guarantee to protect our secrets and the sovereignty of our computer data.
