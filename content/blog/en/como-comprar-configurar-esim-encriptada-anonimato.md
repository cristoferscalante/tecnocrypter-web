# How to buy and configure an encrypted eSIM step by step for complete anonymity

Nowadays, maintaining anonymity in our mobile communications has become an extremely complex task. Traditional telephone operators require mandatory identity verification (KYC) through passports or credit cards, directly associating our number and location with our civil identity. The most advanced and secure solution against this tracking is the **encrypted eSIM**.

An **encrypted eSIM** is a security virtual phone profile that redirects your mobile data through secure network gateways, hiding your geolocation and browsing metadata.

### Step 1: Selection and Purchase without Identification (No-KYC)

To guarantee absolute anonymity, the acquisition process must be free of personal traces:
1. **Use a secure email:** Register using an encrypted and private email such as ProtonMail or Tuta, which does not require prior telephone verification.
2. **Secure Access:** Make your eSIM profile purchase encrypted through the Tor browser or a trusted VPN so that the provider does not log your real physical IP address.
3. **Private payment method:** Avoid bank debit or credit cards. It uses privacy-focused cryptocurrencies, with **Monero (XMR)** being the ideal option because it hides the sender, receiver and the amount of the transaction.

### Step 2: Installing and Configuring the Encrypted Profile

Once the transaction is completed, you will receive an email with a secure QR code containing the encrypted cellular network profile data:
1. On your compatible smartphone (iOS or Android), navigate to *Settings > Mobile networks > Add data plan*.
2. Scan the provided QR code securely.
3. Disable data roaming on your traditional physical SIM to avoid data leaks.
4. Activate the encrypted eSIM as the default line for data use. All cellular traffic will be channeled through the private network's secure tunnel.
