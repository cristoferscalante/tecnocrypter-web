# How to encrypt your data in the cloud: best tools and practices

The cloud is practical, but it also exposes our files if good practices are not used. Encryption is your best ally to maintain privacy.

## Key recommendations
- Use services with **end-to-end encryption**.
- Prefers providers with **Zero Knowledge encryption**.

## Featured Tools
- **Tresorit**: E2E encryption and business policies.
- **Proton Drive**: free, open source.
- **Cryptomator**: encrypts local folders before uploading them.

## Good practices
- Do not reuse passwords.
- Activate two-step verification.
- Make encrypted local backups.

## Conclusion
Encrypting your data before uploading it to the cloud is an essential responsibility to maintain control over your information.
