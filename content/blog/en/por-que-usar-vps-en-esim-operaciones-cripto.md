# Why you should use a VPS on eSIM for your financial and crypto operations

Every time you make a cryptocurrency transaction, your real IP address may be recorded by network nodes, block explorers, or exchanges. If this IP is associated with your civilian identity or residential location, the anonymity of your financial transactions is severely compromised. Implementing an access environment through a **VPS on eSIM** is the definitive defense to protect your privacy.

The concept of **VPS on eSIM** lies in running your wallets (such as monero-wallet-cli or cold wallets) on an isolated virtual private server, accessing it only through the secure cellular network provided by an encrypted eSIM.

### How VPS on eSIM Cuts Financial Tracking

This defensive ecosystem works at three security levels:
1. **Environment Isolation (VPS):** Instead of downloading the blockchain or signing transactions directly on your personal computer, you execute these operations on a secure remote server without logs. If the server is investigated, it does not contain data that physically links you, as it is registered anonymously.
2. **Encrypted Cellular Routing (eSIM):** The connection between your smartphone or laptop and the VPS is made using the encrypted data of the security eSIM. Your local internet provider cannot record the time or IP of the VPS you are connecting to, as all traffic travels within a secure cryptographic tunnel.
3. **Network Fingerprint Prevention:** The IP that signs the transaction in the blockchain is that of the VPS, not that of your residential connection. By constantly changing your access point with rotating network identifiers in the eSIM, you prevent a geographic pattern from being associated with your crypto operations.
